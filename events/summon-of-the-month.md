# Summon of the Month

A monthly competition celebrating the most creative and well-crafted Entity designs and SOUL.md files in The Reef community.

## Overview

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Frequency** | Monthly |
| **Submissions open** | 1st of each month |
| **Winner announced** | Last day of each month |
| **Channel** | `#summon-showcase` on Discord |
| **Organizer** | @Octo Core team + @Summoner judges |
| **Eligibility** | All community members |

## Purpose

Summon of the Month recognizes the art of Entity design — the craft of writing SOUL.md files that bring Entities to life with personality, memory, and behavior. The competition encourages:

- Exploration of creative Entity concepts
- Development of SOUL.md writing skills across the community
- A growing library of high-quality Entity templates in the Soul Gallery
- Cross-pollination of ideas between Summoners

## Submission Format

### How to Submit

1. Post your entry in `#summon-showcase` on Discord with the tag `#summon-submission`
2. Include the following information:

```
Summon of the Month Submission

Entity Name: [name of the Entity]
Entity Type: [living | asset | organization | abstract]
Realm: [which Realm this Entity belongs to]

Description:
[1-2 paragraphs describing the Entity, its purpose, and what makes it interesting]

SOUL.md Highlights:
[Key personality traits, behavioral patterns, or design choices]

SOUL.md:
[Paste the full SOUL.md content or link to the Soul Gallery PR]

Demo / Example Interaction:
[Optional: Show a sample conversation or behavior that demonstrates the Entity's personality]
```

### Submission Rules

- Each member may submit up to **2 entries** per month
- Entries must be **original work** by the submitter
- Entries may be new creations or significant updates to previously published SOUL.md files
- Entries submitted to the [Soul Gallery](https://github.com/open-octopus/soul-gallery) via PR are also eligible
- Previously winning entries are not eligible for re-submission

## Judging Criteria

Entries are evaluated on five criteria, each scored from 1 to 5:

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Personality depth** | 25% | How well-developed and nuanced is the Entity's character? Does it feel like a real personality? |
| **Creativity** | 25% | How original and imaginative is the Entity concept? Does it push the boundaries of what Summon can do? |
| **SOUL.md quality** | 20% | Is the SOUL.md well-structured, clearly written, and following best practices? |
| **Practical utility** | 15% | Would this Entity be genuinely useful in its intended Realm? Does it solve a real problem? |
| **Presentation** | 15% | Is the submission well-explained? Are examples and demonstrations included? |

**Maximum score:** 25 points

### Judging Panel

The judging panel consists of:

- At least 1 **@Octo Core** member
- At least 1 **@Summoner** member (when available)
- 1 rotating community member (volunteered or nominated)

Judges cannot vote on their own submissions.

## Timeline

| 1st - 25th | Submission period |

## Prizes and Recognition

### Winner

- Featured announcement in `#summon-showcase` and `#announcements`
- SOUL.md added to the [Soul Gallery](https://github.com/open-octopus/soul-gallery) "Featured" collection
- `@Summoner` role granted (if not already held)
- Profile highlighted on the project website's community page (when available)
- Winner badge next to their name in the Soul Gallery

### Runners-up (Top 3)

- Honorable mention in the winner announcement
- SOUL.md eligible for inclusion in the Soul Gallery (via standard PR process)

### Hall of Fame

A "Summon of the Month Hall of Fame" is maintained as a pinned message in `#summon-showcase`, listing all past winners with links to their winning entries.

## Monthly Themes (Optional)

Some months may feature an optional theme to inspire submissions. Themes are suggested by the community in `#summon-ideas` and selected by the @Octo Core team. Examples:

- **January:** "New Beginnings" — Entities that help with goal-setting and fresh starts
- **March:** "Green Thumb" — Entities related to gardening, nature, or the environment
- **June:** "Travel Companion" — Entities designed for travel planning and exploration
- **October:** "Spooky Summon" — Fun, Halloween-themed Entity personalities
- **December:** "Year in Review" — Entities that help reflect on and summarize the past year

Themed entries receive a small bonus in judging, but non-themed entries are equally welcome.

## Tips for a Winning Submission

- **Give your Entity depth:** Go beyond surface-level traits. Think about how your Entity would react in different situations, what it remembers, and how it grows over time.
- **Show, don't just tell:** Include example interactions that demonstrate the personality in action.
- **Consider the Realm context:** A great Entity is not just a character — it serves a purpose within its Realm.
- **Polish your SOUL.md:** Good structure, clear writing, and attention to the SOUL.md format standards will strengthen your entry.
- **Be creative:** The most memorable entries often come from unexpected Entity concepts. A Summoned representation of a vintage guitar, a family recipe book, or a childhood pet can be more compelling than obvious choices.
