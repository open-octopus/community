# Community Events — The Reef

This document provides an overview of all recurring community events for The Reef, the official OpenOctopus community. Each event has its own detailed document linked below.

## Event Calendar

| Frequency | Event | Channel | Document |
|-----------|-------|---------|----------|
| Weekly | [Realm of the Week](#realm-of-the-week) | `#showcase` | [realm-of-the-week.md](realm-of-the-week.md) |

## Event Descriptions

### Realm of the Week

A weekly showcase highlighting the best Realm configurations, use cases, or package contributions shared by the community. Every week, the community nominates standout Realm work, and the @Octo Core team selects a winner to be featured.

**When:** Every Friday
**Where:** `#showcase` on Discord
**Details:** [realm-of-the-week.md](realm-of-the-week.md)

### Dev Standup

A bi-weekly voice call for developers to share progress updates, discuss blockers, and demo new features. Open to all contributors — not just @Octo Core and @Tentacler members.

**When:** Every other Tuesday, 17:00 UTC (30 minutes)
**Where:** `Dev Standup` voice channel on Discord
**Details:** [dev-standup.md](dev-standup.md)

### Summon of the Month

A monthly competition celebrating the most creative and well-crafted Entity designs and SOUL.md files. Community members submit their work, and a panel of judges selects the winner.

**When:** Submissions open on the 1st, winner announced on the last day of each month
**Where:** `#summon-showcase` on Discord
**Details:** [summon-of-the-month.md](summon-of-the-month.md)

### AMA Sessions

Ask Me Anything sessions where @Octo Core members, notable contributors, or guest speakers answer community questions about OpenOctopus, AI agents, Realm design, or related topics.

**When:** Scheduled ad hoc (typically once per month or around major releases)
**Where:** `#general` on Discord
**Details:** [ama.md](ama.md)

### Release Announcements

When a new version of OpenOctopus is released, the @Octo Core team publishes a release announcement with changelog highlights, migration notes, and links to the full release notes.

**When:** Per release (roughly every 2-4 weeks)
**Where:** `#announcements` on Discord + GitHub Releases

## Proposing New Events

Community members can propose new events by:

1. Posting in `#ideas` on Discord with a description of the event, proposed format, and frequency
2. If there is interest, an @Octo Core member will help formalize the proposal
3. For significant new recurring events, an RFC may be appropriate (see [GOVERNANCE.md](../governance/GOVERNANCE.md))

## Event Conduct

All community events are subject to the [Code of Conduct](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Participants are expected to be respectful, stay on topic, and contribute constructively. Moderators are present at all events and will enforce community standards.
