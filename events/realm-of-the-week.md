# Realm of the Week

A weekly showcase highlighting outstanding Realm contributions from The Reef community.

## Overview

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Frequency** | Weekly (announced every Friday) |
| **Channel** | `#showcase` on Discord |
| **Organizer** | @Octo Core team |
| **Eligibility** | All community members |

## Purpose

Realm of the Week celebrates community members who create innovative Realm configurations, build useful Realm packages, or demonstrate creative uses of the Realm system. It serves to:

- Inspire others with real-world Realm use cases
- Recognize contributors for their domain expertise
- Surface high-quality Realm packages for the community
- Build a library of Realm examples and best practices

## Nomination Process

### How to Nominate

Anyone can nominate a Realm for the weekly showcase:

1. **Post in `#showcase`** on Discord with the tag `#realm-nomination`
2. Include a description of the Realm and why it deserves recognition
3. Link to the relevant repository, configuration, or screenshots

### Self-Nominations

Self-nominations are welcome and encouraged. Sharing your own work is not boastful — it helps the community learn.

### What Can Be Nominated

- A new Realm package published to RealmHub or the [realms](https://github.com/open-octopus/realms) repository
- A creative Realm configuration shared in `#showcase`
- An interesting use case or workflow built around a Realm
- A significant improvement or update to an existing Realm package

## Selection Process

1. **Nominations collected:** Throughout the week (Monday through Thursday)
2. **Review:** On Friday morning, the @Octo Core team reviews all nominations
3. **Selection criteria:**
   - **Creativity:** Does the Realm solve a problem in a novel way?
   - **Quality:** Is the implementation well-structured, documented, and tested?
   - **Usefulness:** Would other community members benefit from this Realm?
   - **Presentation:** Is the nomination clearly explained with good examples?
4. **Announcement:** The winner is announced on Friday in `#showcase`

## Showcase Post Template

The @Octo Core team uses the following template for the weekly announcement:

```
Realm of the Week

This week's featured Realm: [Realm Name]
Author: @[username]

[Brief description of what the Realm does and why it stands out]

Highlights:
- [Key feature or design decision 1]
- [Key feature or design decision 2]
- [Key feature or design decision 3]

Check it out: [link to repository or configuration]

Congratulations @[username]! Your Realm expertise is an inspiration to The Reef.

Nominations for next week are open! Share your Realm in #showcase with #realm-nomination.
```

## Recognition

Winners receive:

- A featured post in `#showcase` using the template above
- A shoutout in the next `#announcements` community update
- Eligibility for the `@Realm Builder` role (if not already held)
- Their Realm added to the "Community Highlights" section of the project website (when available)

## Past Winners

A log of past Realm of the Week winners is maintained in a pinned message in `#showcase`. Each entry includes the date, Realm name, author, and a link to the showcase post.

## Tips for a Strong Nomination

- **Include context:** Explain what problem the Realm solves and for whom
- **Show, don't just tell:** Include screenshots, configuration snippets, or demo videos
- **Document your Realm:** A well-written REALM.md file significantly strengthens a nomination
- **Highlight what makes it unique:** What does your Realm do differently from existing solutions?
